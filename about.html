About Us

ISIRI is dedicated to advancing Indian agriculture by producing and supplying superior quality seeds. Founded with a passion for farming, we have become a trusted partner for farmers seeking to boost their productivity and ensure sustainable growth. Our commitment to research and innovation drives us to provide seeds that thrive and prosper.

Driven by the vision of “Quality First,” Isiri Seeds combines Indian agricultural excellence with global standards of quality and integrity. Our production hubs in key seed-growing regions are optimized for climate suitability, labour efficiency, and agronomic precision—making us a preferred partner for companies sourcing hybrid seeds from India. With a focus on sustainability, consistency, and long-term collaboration, Isiri Seeds continues to empower global agriculture by delivering high quality seeds that help companies’ deliver best seeds to their farmers and thrive worldwide.
